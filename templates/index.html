What can visitors do?

  • Visitors can search for songs.
  • If you want to rate and review a song, you have to log in to your account.
  • If you do not have an account with us yet, you can register Here.

    • What can registered users do?

  • Register users can also search for songs.
  • In addition that, you can rate and review a song.
  • You can also manage friend requests and follow other users.

    • Where can we search for songs?

  • You can search on the Search Song page.

    • How to Rate and Review a Song?

  • First, search a song you want to rate or review on the Search Song page.
  • After you get the search results, click on the song title.
  • Detailed information about song will appears on the next page.
  • At the end of the page, there is a rating and review section.
    • (Note: Rating and review can only be perform by registered users.)
